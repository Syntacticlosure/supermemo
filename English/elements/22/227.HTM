1.to put tasks, problems, etc. in order of importance, so that you can deal with the most important first 按重要性排序；划分优先顺序

You should make a list of all the jobs you have to do and prioritize them.你应该把所有要做的事情列出来，并按轻重缓急排序。

2.to treat sth. as being more important than other things 优先处理

The organization was formed to prioritize the needs of older peole.

这个机构是为优先满足老年人的需要而成立的。
