By derivation, a leader (agogos) of the people (demos), a demagogue today is actually one who attempts, in essence, to mislead the people, a politician who foments (挑起，激起，煽动,fo) discontent (不满,from 'content') among the masses,rousing them to [...](狂热，from 'fever') by wild oratory (演讲术，from ōrare "to speak, pray, plead"), in an attempt to be voted into office.
