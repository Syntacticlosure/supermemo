tact (n.)
1650s, "sense of touch or feeling" (with an isolated instance, tacþe from c. 1200), from Latin tactus "a touch, handling, sense of touch,".

[...] : careful not to say or do anything that will annoy or upset other people 圆通的；得体的；不得罪人的

tactless : saying or doing things that are likely to annoy or to upset other people 言行不得体的；得罪人的；不圆通的；没策略的

tactual : pertaining to the sense of touch,触觉的
