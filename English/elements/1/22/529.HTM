By derivation, a leader (agogos) of the people (demos), a [...]today is actually one who attempts, in essence, to mislead the people, a politician who foments (挑起，激起，煽动,fo) discontent (不满,from 'content') among the masses,rousing them to fever pitch (狂热，from 'fever') by wild oratory (演讲术，from ōrare "to speak, pray, plead"), in an attempt to be voted into office.
