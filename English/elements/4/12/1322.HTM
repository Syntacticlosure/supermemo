It is widely accepted (人们广泛接受) that "Never give up", a strong and positive life attitude, is the key for individual success on the grounds (由于,phrase from ground) that permanent persistence may boost (增强，增长,b) our confidence, strengthen our will (意志,w), and provide us a second chance（第二次机会,phrase from chance) for success when we are in hardship(艰难，困苦，拮据,from hard).
