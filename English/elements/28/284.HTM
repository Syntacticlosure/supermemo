1. ~ （in sth) (of a solid) to mix with a liquid and become part of it 溶解

Salt dissolves in water. 盐溶于水。

~ sth in sth

2. to officially end a marriage, business agreement or parliament 解除婚姻关系；终止商业协议；解散议会

3. to disappear; to make sth disappear 消除；使消失；消散

When the ambulance had gone, the crowd dissolved. 救护车离开后，人群便离开了。
