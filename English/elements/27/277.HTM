1. a contagious disease spreads by people touching with each other （疾病） 接触传染的

Scarlet fever is highly contagious. 猩红热的接触传染性很强。

(figurative) His enthusiasm is contagious. 他的热情富有感染力。

a contagious laugh 有感染力的笑声

2.(not usually before noun) if a person is contagious, they have a disease that can spread to other people by touch. 患接触性传染病。
